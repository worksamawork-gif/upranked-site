# Brand Voice — upranked.io

## The Voice in Three Words

**Authoritative. Surgical. GCC-native.**

---

## Tone Principles

### What upranked.io sounds like
- A senior GCC market strategist talking directly to a peer
- Specific, named markets and sectors (not "MENA", not "the region")
- Confident claims backed by a named framework (APEX™)
- Short sentences. No filler.
- First person is Sama Alaa. Brand voice is her voice.

### What it never sounds like
- "We are a full-service digital agency..."
- "Data-driven strategies for sustainable growth"
- "Our team of experts..."
- Passive voice about results
- Western agency speak applied to a GCC context

---

## Vocabulary Guide

### Use these words
- APEX Framework™ (always with ™)
- GCC (not "Middle East", not "MENA")
- Sama Alaa (founder, always by name)
- upranked.io (lowercase, always)
- APEX Diagnostic (the free call CTA)
- Growth Intelligence (the service pillar name)
- Authority, Topical depth, E-E-A-T
- Dubai, Riyadh, Kuwait City, Manama (specific cities, not country-level)

### Avoid these words
- "Full-service"
- "Innovative" / "cutting-edge"
- "Results-driven" / "data-driven"
- "MENA" / "Middle East" (use GCC or specific country)
- "Leverage" (as a verb)
- Em dashes (use comma, colon, or period instead)
- "Holistic approach"
- "Synergy"

---

## CTA Language Patterns

The primary CTA is always a consultation booking, framed around value delivery:

**Good CTAs:**
- "Book Your Free APEX Diagnostic"
- "Book a Free 30-Minute Strategy Call"
- "Get a Clear, Actionable Plan for Your GCC Market"
- "Talk to Sama Alaa — Free, No Pitch"

**Never:**
- "Get Started"
- "Learn More"
- "Contact Us"
- "Submit"

## Content Voice by Section Type

### Hero Headlines
Short, declarative, market-specific.
> "Medical SEO for Dubai Clinics That Want to Rank First — Not Paid Second."
> "Industrial B2B SEO for GCC Manufacturers and Suppliers."

### Body Paragraphs
Explains the problem before the solution. Never starts with "We". Maximum 3 sentences before a break.

### Key Intelligence / Callouts
The most important insight, distilled. Written like a briefing, not a summary.
> "Google's 2025 algorithm updates have systematically demoted thin AI-generated content in GCC markets — rewarding businesses that demonstrate real sector expertise over agencies that recycle Western playbooks."

### FAQ Answers
Direct. No hedging. Written as if answering a real client question in a meeting.

### Blog Posts
Author voice is Sama Alaa. Byline should appear. Content is specific, contrarian where warranted, and always tied to named GCC markets. No generic SEO advice that could have been written by any agency.
